Long-term Program 

I. Length of schooling
　　　1. Long-term student: half a year (one semester), above one year (including). There are two semesters in each year, the spring semester starts at the end of February, the autumn semester starts at the beginning of September. 
　　　2. Short-term student: students with certain Chinese foundation will be arranged to join the long-term Chinese class of relevant level after test.
II. Application time
　　　1. Long-term student: the application of spring semester ends at 28th February each year, the application of autumn semester ends at 31st July each year. (For the students who are not in China, the application of spring semester ends at 15th February each year, the application of autumn semester ends at 15th July each year.)
　　　2. Short-term student: any time is available.
III. Application qualification:
　　　Foreigners whose age ranging from 16years old(including) and 65 years old(including).
IV. Application procedures
　　　1. Online application→ International Exchange Center exams and verifies and replies the application results by emails→ the applicant emails the remittance receipt of the application fee to iec@zjut.edu.cn → After verification, International Exchange Center will send the Letter of Acceptance and JW202 Visa Application Form to the applicant→ The applicant go to the nearest Chinese Embassy (Consulate) for the application of Study (X) Visa or Visiting (F) Visa, the applicant can also apply for the Tourist (L) Visa, the other procedures can be handled after enrollment.
　　　2. On-site application (address: South 224, East Teaching Building, Zhejiang University of Technology, Zhaohui Campus)
V. Learning contents
　1) Elementary I and II
　　1. Compulsory courses: 
　　Elementary Comprehensive Chinese (10 class hours each week)
　　Elementary Spoken Chinese(4 class hours each week)
　　Elementary Chinese Listening(4 class hours each week)
　　Elementary Chinese Reading(2 class hours each week)
　　2. Selected courses: Chinese Calligraphy, Taijiquan and Standard Pronunciation
　2) Intermediate I and II
　　1. Compulsory courses: 
　　Intermediate Comprehensive Chinese
　　Intermediate Spoken Chinese
　　Intermediate Chinese Listening
　　Intermediate Chinese Reading
　　2. Selected courses: Audio-Visual-Oral Chinese, Idioms in Spoken Chinese, An Introduction to Chinese Food, Folk Customs in Hangzhou, Chinese Calligraphy, HSK mentoring and Shadow Boxing
　3) Advanced class 
　　1. Compulsory course: 
　　Advanced Comprehensive Chinese
　　Advanced Spoken Chinese
　　Advanced Chinese Listening
　　Newspaper Reading
2. Selected courses: Writing, News Listening, Audio-Visual-Oral Chinese, Idioms in Spoken Chinese, China's Actual Conditions, Chinese History, An Introduction to Chinese Food, Folk Customs in Hangzhou, HSK mentoring and Shadow Boxing

Short-term Programs

Summer Course for Short-Term Chinese Language Training of Zhejiang University of Technology is centered on the integrative Chinese language teaching for overseas students through short-term intensive training of Chinese. The course for Chinese language teaching starts from Monday to Friday (3-4 credit hours per day), and 4 or 8 weeks of class hours will be set according to the requirements of the students. The student will be arranged to study in the class of a corresponding Chinese level according to his/her HSK test result at the beginning of the semester.

I. Time for course commencement  

1) From 4 July to 29 July, the graded test is on 1 July.

2) From 1 August to 26 August, the graded test is on 29 July.

3) From 4 July to 26 August, the graded test is on 1 July.

★  Summer course for Chinese language training is also available for the group registration. The time for course commencement and detailed timetable can be negotiated with the International Exchange Center according to the requirements of the student. .

II. Time for registration

From now to 24 June.

Specialty Program

Undergraduate

I. Specialty

1. Specialty taught in Chinese: see more details in Specialty taught in Chinese.doc

2. Specialty taught in English: see more details in  Specialty taught in English.doc

 

II. Length of schooling

4 years（5 years for Architecture）

 

III. Period of application

Deadline for application is 20th of August.

 

IV. Term starts: 

At the beginning of September (the specific opening time is subject to the Letter of Acceptance)

 

V. Application requirements:

1. The applicant, who is above 18 years old (including), has graduated from high school, has achieved above level 4(including) in HSK for sciences studies and achieved above level 4 (including) in HSK for arts studies, can apply for our school.

2. The high school graduate without certificate of HSK can apply for our Try-out Courses after approval by interview, the validity of trial period is one year.

3. Programs in English: 1) English is the native language/official language; or 2) IELTS5.0 or TOEFL 60; or 3) Studied courses taught in English in high school or college; or 4) English level admitted by ZJUT.

 

VI. Application materials:

1. Application Form for Undergraduate program.doc or application online

2. The copy of passport

3. 3 recent photos (color photo in white background, 35mm* 45mm)

4. The notarized high school diploma and the copy of transcript (The documents in other language except Chinese and English shall enclosed with the notarized translated version in Chinese or English)

5、This year’s high school graduates are expected to show anticipated graduates proof, and show the high school certificate after admission

6、The copy of HSK transcript（for Chinese programs）

7、Students whose native language is not English should provide proof of English proficiency or the copy of IELTS/TOEFL transcript (for English programs)  

8、Physical examination form（in 6 month）

 

VII. Way of application 

1. Online application→ International Exchange Center exams and verifies and replies the application results by emails→ the applicant emails the remittance receipt of the application fee to iec@zjut.edu.cn → After verification, International Exchange Center will send the Letter of Acceptance and JW202 Visa Application Form to the applicant→ The applicant go to the nearest Chinese Embassy (Consulate) for the application of Study (X) Visa or Visiting (F) Visa, the applicant can also apply for the Tourist (L) Visa, the other procedures can be handled after enrollment.
2. On-site application (address: South 224, East Teaching Building, Zhejiang University of Technology, Zhaohui Campus)
