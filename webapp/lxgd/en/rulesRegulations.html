Visa Residence Permit 

I. Application for study permit 

　　The full-time overseas students can apply for the permit of studying in China with the issuance of a reference letter of our school. 
　　1. If the applicant is here in China, he/she shall sign up at the International Exchange Center of Zhejiang University of Technology Zhaohui Campus with Registration Voucher of Residence for Visitors from Overseas (for the one that has permanent residence in Hangzhou) one month before the expiration of the current permit. The residence permit can only be obtained after payment of the tuition and registration fee.
　　2. If the applicant is out of China, he/she should first contact with staff of our center by email, and then remit the registration fee and part of tuition to the account of our school after submitting the required materials. We will send the Letter of Acceptance and Form JW202 to the applicant upon confirmation. And the applicant can obtain the study permit or tourist visa for enrollment at our school by submitting the aforesaid letter and form to the overseas Chinese Embassy in person. The residence permit application can only be transacted with our assistance after the full payment of tuition.


II. The working hour and address of Division of Exit-Entry Administration of Hangzhou Municipal Public Security Bureau

　　Working hour: from Monday to Friday (8:30-12:00, 14:00-17:00; summer time: 14:30-17:00)
　　Address: 
　　No.35 Huaguang Rd., Hangzhou
　　 (next to the hall of Hangzhou Municipal Public Security Bureau)
　　 

III. Application materials

　　1. The official document (shall be noted with the applicant’s basic information, application reasons, contents and validity), Letter of Acceptance and Form JW201or Form JW202 issued by the school applied for;
　　2. The original and copy of valid passport and visa;
　　3. The original and copy of Certificate of Temporary Residence Registration (Certificate of Hotel Accommodation or Temporary Resident Voucher issued by the Police Station) (shall be declared annually if the residential address dosen't change);
　　4. One full-face bareheaded color photo in white background of 2 inches size;
　　5. As for the first-time application of residence permit for more than one year, Health Certificate for Foreigners (minors under 18 years old are excluded) shall be provided;
　　6. Fill in the Application Form of Visa and Residence Permit for Foreigners;
　　7. Other necessary materials required by the public security organ.
　　Note: 1. The Health Certificate shall be issued by Zhejiang Entry-Exit Inspection and Quarantine Bureau (International Travel Healthcare Center). Tel: 87852407; 87852408; Address: 
　　No. 228 Zhonghe Middle Road, Hangzhou
　　.
　　 2. As for the first-time application, the applicant shall go to the Division of Exit-Entry Administration of Hangzhou Municipal Public Security Bureau for the transaction in person.

Attendance Regulations

1.  Students’ attendance will be calculated once a month. Those whose attendance less than 50% will be reported to the immigration, which might be ended with visa cancelled.

2.  One may only enroll for a new semester when he/she has completed an attendance of at least 60% to all of his/her classes during the present semester.

3. A study certificate may only be awarded to the students whose attendance is HIGHER than 50%, A certification for travel may only be approved to the students whose attendance is higher than 60%.

4.  A relative certification is NEEDED when a student needs to take a day off of school (Sick leaves will NEED relative certifications from the doctors/hospitals).  leaves for personal reasons will be counted in the absence.

5.  Students whose attendance less than 50% won’t be able to take the middle/final exams.

Hostel Regulations

1.  There are two kinds of rooms, which cost 24 RMB/day/person and 40 RMB/day/person. Two students share a room and there is no single room. Each room has a bathroom and there is a kitchen and a laundry on every floor.

2.  IF a new student wants to live in dormitory, it is a MUST for the person to assure that he/she is going to stay for at least 6 month. The student needs to pay the full amount of the accommodation fee and 800 RMB of deposit fee. IF the student needs to move out earlier, it is a MUST for him/her to ask the nearby police station to certify a paper of REGISTRATION VOCHER OF RESIDENCE FOR VISITORS FROM OVERSEAS to prove a new settled property has been found and ready to move in. The absence of proof will not allow the refund of accommodation fee and deposit fee.
